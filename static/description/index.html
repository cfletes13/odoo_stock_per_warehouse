Odoo Stock Per Warehouse

Show Product Stock Per Warehouse on Odoo Website

Check Product Stock Availability on Website With A Click

Customers can Click on the Check availability Button to know the availability of stock

Cross shows the unavailability of product in the warehouse. Right Check shows the availability of the product in the warehouse.

Define the Rules to Show the Stock on Website

Show the availability of stock on the basis of On-hand quantity or Forecasted quantity.

Configuration to Show the Product Stock

You can enable or disable the configuration for all products. Or, you can also configure the rules at product level.

You can also restrict the non-logged in users to view the stock of the products in the warehouse from the Odoo Backend.

Multi Website Compatible

Odoo Stock Per Warehouse is compatible with multi website. Hence, you can configure the stock availability uniquely for each website.
